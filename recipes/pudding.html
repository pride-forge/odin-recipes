Decription

Pudding is a simple, creamy dessert that's easy to make and endlessly customizable. It starts by whisking together milk, sugar, and a thickening agent like cornstarch or eggs over medium heat. As it warms, the mixture thickens into a smooth, velvety texture. Flavors like vanilla, chocolate, or banana can be added depending on your preference.

Once thickened, the pudding is poured into serving dishes and chilled to set. The result is a rich, comforting treat that can be enjoyed on its own or topped with whipped cream, fruit, or cookies. Itâ€™s a quick dessert with classic appeal.
