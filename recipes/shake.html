Decription

A shake is a cold, creamy drink that’s quick to make and perfect for a sweet treat. Just blend together milk, ice cream, and your flavor of choice—like chocolate syrup, strawberries, or bananas—until smooth and frothy. You can adjust the thickness by adding more milk or ice cream.

Pour it into a glass, top with whipped cream if you like, and enjoy. It’s a simple, satisfying drink that hits the spot every time.

Ingredients

  • Milk
  • Favourite fruit
  • Ice
  • Honey

Steps

  1. Prepare ingredients
  2. Blend all ingredients
  3. Pour into high glass
